A farmer is fencing off space for his chickens using two rolls of wire fence that are equal in length. One entire roll of wire fence is used to enclose space A in the shape of a regular polygon with 8 sides, and the entire second roll is used to enclose space B in the shape of a regular polygon with 5 sides. If each side of B is 6 feet longer than each side of A, what is the length, in feet, of one roll of wire fence?

Exactly one roll of fence is used to create each of the two spaces: space A, the regular 8-sided polygon (octagon) and space B, the regular 5-sided polygon (pentagon). All of the sides of a regular polygon have the same length, so one roll of fence creates space A with 8 equal sides, while the other creates space B with 5 equal sides.
